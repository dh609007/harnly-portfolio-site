Metropolitan Museum of Art

Promotional Materials

These are promotional materials designed for the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s show, Leon Golub: Raw Nerve. Using abstract vertical lines to represent the energy and freneticism of Golub’s work, the colors are taken directly from Golub’s works; Gigantomachy II and Tete de Chaval II. The materials themselves consist of a simple poster, a postcard and a handout meant to be given to the museum’s guests once they are inside the venerable museum.
